Hamburger Stroganoff Recipe

What is Hamburger Stroganoff?

Hamburger Stroganoff is a hearty, comforting dish featuring ground beef in a creamy sauce made with sour cream, beef broth, and mushrooms, served over a bed of egg noodles. This quick and easy recipe is a family favorite, perfect for a busy weeknight meal with its rich flavors and simple preparation.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onions and garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Flour
  • Beef broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Sour cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Egg noodles
  • Fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Brown the beef: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Saute onions and garlic: Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the beef. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onions are softened and fragrant
  3. Cook the mushrooms: Stir in the mushrooms and cook until they soften, about 5 minutes.
  4. Make the sauce: Sprinkle the flour over the beef and mushrooms, stirring to combine. Slowly pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Add Worcestershire sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer: Let the mixture simmer for 5-10 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  6. Add sour cream: Remove the skillet from heat and stir in the sour cream until the sauce is creamy and smooth.
  7. Serve: Spoon the Hamburger Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.
